Quick answer

For most people on a budget, the Conair Turbo ExtremeSteam Handheld Steamer offers the best balance of power, heat-up speed, and ease of use. If you want a slightly nicer finish and are willing to spend a bit more, the Rowenta Pure Steam Handheld Steamer is a worthy upgrade; the Steamfast SF-717 Mini Steam Travel Steamer is the top pick for portability.

A clothes steamer can banish wrinkles in seconds, save your delicate fabrics from an iron’s heat, and make you look put-together without ever pulling out an ironing board. For budget shoppers, the market is flooded with cheap handhelds, but not all of them get hot enough to actually de-wrinkle a cotton shirt, and some leak or spit water. The key is to look for a steamer that heats up quickly, produces a steady, dry steam, and feels comfortable to hold – you want something that will last through a few years of morning touch-ups without breaking the bank.
