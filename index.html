How we choose our picks

We do not run our own lab tests. Instead, we dig into the long-term owner feedback across thousands of verified reviews, survey hands-on reviews from trusted publications, and track what actually breaks (leaky tanks, weak steam after a month, flimsy handles). We weigh real-world durability and ease of use far more heavily than marketing claims. We give extra scrutiny to anti-drip claims, heat-up times, and water-tank design, the three things that annoy owners most. If a model consistently draws complaints about dribbling or losing power over time, it does not make our list, no matter how shiny the marketing photos look.
